Data Storage and Databases

Data Storage

  • Data storage is where information is kept for later use.
  • It can be physical, like hard drives, or virtual, such as cloud storage.

Databases

  • Databases are organized collections of data, structured for easy access and management.
  • They enable efficient storage, retrieval, and manipulation of data.
